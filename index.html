Frequently Asked Questions

What documents do I need to rent a car?

+

To rent a car, you'll need:
- A valid driving license
- Proof of identity (passport or ID card)
- Credit card for security deposit
- Proof of address (utility bill or bank statement)

What is your cancellation policy?

+

Free cancellation up to 48 hours before pickup. Cancellations within 48 hours may incur a charge of one day's rental fee.

Is insurance included in the rental price?

+

Basic insurance is included in all rentals. Additional coverage options are available at pickup for enhanced protection.

What is your fuel policy?

+

We operate a full-to-full fuel policy. Cars are provided with a full tank, and should be returned full to avoid refueling charges.

Do you offer one-way rentals?

+

Yes, one-way rentals are available between our major city locations. Additional fees may apply based on the distance.

About Rent-a-Car

Your Premium Car Rental Service in the UK

Founded in 1995 in London, Rent-a-Car has grown to become one of the UK's most trusted premium car rental services. With over 25 years of experience, we've built our reputation on exceptional service, an extensive fleet of luxury vehicles, and a commitment to customer satisfaction.

Our Mission

To provide our customers with an unparalleled car rental experience, combining luxury vehicles with outstanding service and competitive prices. We strive to make every journey memorable, whether it's a business trip or a leisure adventure.

Why Choose Us?

  • Premium fleet of latest model vehicles
  • Nationwide coverage with locations in major UK cities
  • 24/7 roadside assistance
  • Flexible rental terms
  • Competitive pricing with no hidden fees
  • Professional, multilingual staff

Our Commitment

We're committed to providing environmentally conscious travel options, with a growing fleet of hybrid and electric vehicles. Our dedication to sustainability goes hand in hand with our promise to deliver excellence in service and vehicle quality.
